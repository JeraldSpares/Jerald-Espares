## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **BSIT senior** focused on shipping software that businesses actually use — not just classroom demos. My work spans **AI-powered systems**, **web platforms**, and **mobile applications**, with clients ranging from local cafés to enterprise dealerships.

- 🔭 Currently building **CafeConnect**, an online ordering platform for Hebrew's Cafe
- 🌱 Deepening my skills in **Next.js 16**, **Supabase**, and **AI agents**
- 💡 I care about clean code, sensible UX, and systems that solve real problems
- 🤝 Open to internships, freelance work, and collaboration
- 📍 Based in **Makati City, Philippines**

#### 🍵 [CafeConnect — Online Ordering & Management System](https://github.com/JeraldSpares/CafeConnect-Online-Ordering-Management-System)

`Next.js 16` · `Supabase` · `TypeScript` · `Tailwind CSS` · `PostgreSQL`

---

#### 🚗 [DealerHub — AI-Powered Vehicle Financing](https://github.com/JeraldSpares/DealerHub-AI-Powered-Vehicle-Financing-System-Nissan-Philippines)

`AI` · `HTML` · `Financial Automation`
